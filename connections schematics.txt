Smart Wireless Charger - Wiring Schematic
=========================================

Components:
- ESP32 Dev Module
- ACS712 5A Current Sensor
- 5V Relay Module
- Wireless Charging Transmitter Coil
- 5V Power Supply

Pin Connections:
================

ESP32                  → Component
---------------------    -------------------
GPIO 34 (ADC)         → ACS712 OUT (Signal)
GPIO 26               → Relay IN (Control)
5V (VIN)              → Relay VCC
GND                   → Relay GND
GND                   → ACS712 GND
5V                    → ACS712 VCC

Power Flow:
===========
Power Supply 5V ──► Relay COM
Relay NO      ──► ACS712 IP+ 
ACS712 IP-    ──► Transmitter Coil Positive
Transmitter Coil Negative ──► GND

ACS712 Wiring:
==============
- VCC  → 5V
- GND  → GND
- OUT  → ESP32 GPIO 34
- IP+  → From Relay NO
- IP-  → To Transmitter Coil +

Notes:
- Relay is Normally Open (NO)
- ACS712 measures current going to the transmitter coil
- When phone is detected (current spike), relay turns ON
- Common GND between ESP32, Relay, ACS712 and Power Supply
